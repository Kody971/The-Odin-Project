Fried Chicken Garlic

Ingredients

  • Tapioca flour
  • Garlic
  • Palm oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix water with tapioca flour, crushed garlic and salt for marination as desired
  2. Wait minimum 1 hour
  3. Fry the chicken with low heat till medium rare and drain
  4. Wait for 10 minutes
  5. Fry again with high heat till brown crysp and drain
  6. Serve while still crysp
